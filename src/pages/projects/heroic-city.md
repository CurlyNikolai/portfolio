## Overview

Multiplayer platform including various party games, a customizable home for players, and eventually an impact aspect with which players can affect the world for the better.

## My Role

* Game mechanics: Player home furnishing system, player movement, camera behaviour, and more.
* Game flow: Scene handling, event handling, implementation of bomber and soccer games, and more.
* In-game purchasing: Implementation of in-game shops handling both virtual and real currency.
* UI: Player storage, in-game shops, player prompts, trophy roads, quest logs, and more.
* AI: Finite state machines for soccer and bomber game.
