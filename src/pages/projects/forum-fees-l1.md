## Overview

The FORUM (Far-infrared Outgoing Radiation Understanding and Monitoring) satellite is an Earth observation satellite meant to increase knowledge of Earth's radiation budget by measuring a specific range of the infrared spectrum ("far-infrared") that has not been measured from space before.
