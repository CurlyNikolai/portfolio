## Overview

Prototype for a top-down fast paced shooter. Focus of testing was on bullet mechanics and snappy movement. 

## Demo Instructions

* Move: WASD
* Aim/Rotate: Mouse
* Select Weapon: 1/2/3
* Shoot: Mouse Left
* Dash: Left Shift
