## Overview

Puzzle/action game created for the Game Project course at Aalto university that became the runner-up at the Bit1 2022 competition. The objective of the game is to get through each room filled with enemies presenting a puzzly challenge.

## Synopsis

Killed by your father, resurrected by the God of Death and granted the ability to possess the bodies of your fallen enemies, embark on an epic journey of revenge. 

Escape the halls of the underworld and ascend the ancient tower at the top of the world where your father abodes. Use your reactiveness and wit to clear room after room in this babylovecraftian environment
