## Overview

As a doctoral researcher at the University of Helsinki, I studied radiation damage in high entropy alloys, in the context of structural components for nuclear reactors. All studies were purely computational, using molecular dynamics to simulate prolonged irradiation in the investigated materials. Results from the studies were published in several peer-reviewed articles, as well as my doctoral dissertation “Radiation Damage in High Entropy Alloys”.
